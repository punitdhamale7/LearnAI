Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI adapt to my learning style?

+

Our AI analyzes your quiz performance, time spent on topics, and learning patterns. Based on this data, it adjusts content difficulty, recommends relevant materials, and creates a personalized learning path that matches your pace and preferences.

Can I switch plans anytime?

+

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges or credits accordingly.

Do I get certificates after completing courses?

+

Pro and Premium members receive verified certificates upon course completion. These certificates can be shared on LinkedIn and added to your resume to showcase your skills.

Is there a free trial available?

+

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for the Pro plan. No credit card required. You'll get full access to all Pro features during the trial period.

What if I need help during a course?

+

All members have access to our community forum. Pro members get email support within 24 hours, while Premium members enjoy priority 24/7 support and 1-on-1 mentorship sessions.

Can I access courses on mobile devices?

+

Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. Learn anytime, anywhere at your convenience.
